## Useful Design Signals

- Keep the site restrained: large quiet surfaces, one strong product image, spare typography.
- Pull colors from materials rather than decoration: rose plaster, cobalt tile, leather cognac, cedar brown, brass patina.
- Use texture as hierarchy: paper grain, plaster, leather, ceramic glaze, and matte shadows can do more work than borders.
- Avoid generic ornament. When using pattern, make it specific: zellige edge, tadelakt wall, carved cedar relief.
- Product shots should feel placed in the world, not isolated in a white catalog void.
- For implementation, prioritize 16, 17, and 18. They make the site feel like shopping inside a Marrakech store instead of reading a dry catalog.
- The interface should feel spatial: shelves, counter, drawers, hotspots, material trays, and products placed in the boutique.
- Keep it ecommerce-functional: clear navigation, product cards, size/color controls, prices in MAD, WhatsApp ordering, French and Arabic support.
